Main Ideas A financial derivative is defined in terms of some underlying asset which already exists on the market. We thus want to price the derivative in a way that is consistent with the underlying prices given by the market. We are not trying to compute the price of the derivative in some "absolute" sense. The idea instead is to determine the price of the derivative in terms of the market prices of the underlying assets.
